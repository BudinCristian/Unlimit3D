About Us


Here are some things about us:
We are the Deviants, a group made of 6 enthusiastic students from the ninth, tenth and eleventh grade and the best mentor out there. Not only the school we all belong to unites us but also the passion and devotion for coding and design. We will work together in the next six months to create a useful application for anyone regardless of the domain in which they work. We are aware of the state of the ecosystem and so we want to be part of the solution to save the planet.
